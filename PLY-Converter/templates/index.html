Smoothing Level Guide
Light Smoothing
  • 3 iterations of Laplacian smoothing
  • Preserves original geometry
  • Best for: Scanned objects with minor noise
  • Processing time: Fast
Medium Smoothing
  • Taubin + Laplacian combination
  • Volume-preserving smoothing
  • Best for: Most PLY files
  • Processing time: Moderate
High Smoothing
  • Loop subdivision + advanced smoothing
  • Creates very smooth surfaces
  • Best for: Organic shapes, sculptures
  • Processing time: Longer
Ultra Smoothing
  • Multiple subdivision passes
  • Glass-like surface finish
  • Best for: Display models, visualization
  • Processing time: Longest
